Our Method

THE 1-DAY INSTALLATION

We use polyaspartic technology which cures faster and stronger than traditional epoxy. Back in your garage in 24 hours.

1

Prep & Grind

Diamond grinding the concrete to open pores for maximum adhesion. Fixing cracks and divots.

2

Base Coat

Applying the industrial-grade tinted base coat that bonds deep into the concrete substrate.

3

Flake Broadcast

Full saturation of vinyl flakes for texture, durability, and that premium terrazzo look.

4

Clear Coat

Sealing with UV-stable Polyaspartic. scrape, vacuum, and seal. Walk on it in 4-6 hours.
